This peer's status #(peerStatus)# Virgin - You have not published your peer seed yet. This happens automatically, just wait. While you have this status you are not allowed to search other peers. :: Junior - You cannot be reached from outside. A possible reason is that you are behind a firewall, NAT or Router. But you can search the internet using the other peers' global index on your own search page.
