Maximum URLs currently assigned
to one cached word:		 #[maxURLinWordCache]# This is the maximum size of URLs assigned to a single word cache entry. If this is a big number, it shows that the caching works efficiently.

Maximum number of Word Caches, low limit: This is is the number of word indexes that shall be held in the ram cache during indexing.
